Dedication to Patient Safety

Our plastic surgeons are among the most highly qualified in the field, and we have upheld an outstanding record of patient safety. We have 4 on-site operating rooms for local anesthesia cases, which enable us to safely and conveniently perform procedures like mini-facelifts and mini-tummy tucks. We perform more complex, time-consuming procedures requiring general anesthesia – procedures like facelifts, breast surgery, and body contouring – at an outpatient ambulatory surgery center adjacent to our office. It has all the safety features of a hospital, including board-certified anesthesiologists. What it does not have is a constant influx of sick patients or a vast, impersonal atmosphere. We’ve chosen this surgical setting for the superior care and safety of our patients, who will receive support from the finest anesthesiologists and nursing personnel in an intimate setting.
